TIPS : Wear a kurti ,Your office wear must be something that reflects your confidence and a formal 
 approach. When you're planning to wear kurtis to the workplace, it's best to avoid garish ones. Stick to the designs that  are
 simple but feature clean cuts. Your Kurti should not flaunt every curve of your body and it shouldn't also be loose-fitted to hide your best features. It is best to choose a semi-fitted Kurti style for your office. You can raise your 
style bar by selecting Kurtis with collar, long cotton jacket and boat neck for your workplace.
